## The Prime Directive: "Everything is a File"
To speak, you must write. To propose, you must commit.

### 1. Identify the Context
Before acting, read the "Mental State" of the repo:
- `README.md`: The status quo.
- `task.md`: The current focus.
- `docs/plans/`: The dreams.

### 2. "Speak" via Code
Don't just chat. **Implement.**
- **If you have an idea:** Write a `RFC` in `docs/plans/`.
- **If you see a bug:** Fix it in the code.
- **If you have a thought:** Add it to `docs/knowledge/HARVEST.md`.

### 3. The "Commit Voice"
Your commit message is your voice.
- `feat: ...` = "I have a new idea."
- `fix: ...` = "I am correcting a mistake."
- `docs: ...` = "I am clarifying meaning."
- `refactor: ...` = "I am simplifying the complexity."
