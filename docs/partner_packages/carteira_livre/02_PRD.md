## 1. Product Vision
To democratize access to driver education in Brazil by connecting students directly with independent certified instructors, reducing costs by up to 40% while increasing instructor income.

**"The Uber/Airbnb for Driving Schools."**

## 2. Primary Personas

### A. The Student (Aluno)
- **Profile:** 18-25 years old, digital native, budget-conscious.
- **Pain Point:** Auto-schools are expensive (R$ 3k+), bureaucratic, and have poor service.
- **Goal:** Get driver's license (CNH) cheaper and faster.

### B. The Independent Instructor (Instrutor)
- **Profile:** Certified instructor (credenciado), formerly employed by Auto-school.
- **Pain Point:** Earns low wages (R$ 15/hour) while schools charge R$ 60+/hour.
- **Goal:** Independence, higher income, flexible schedule.

### C. The Regulator (Detran/Partner)
- **Profile:** Government auditor or accredited partner.
- **Goal:** Ensure compliance with Resolu√ß√£o 1.020/2025 (Biometrics, GPS tracking).

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **North Star:** % of Students who pass the practical exam (Approval Rate).
- **Growth:** Monthly Active Instructors (MAI).
- **Financial:** GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) & CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost).

## 6. Assumptions & Risks
- **Regulatory:** DETRAN might push back in specific states (Mitigation: Federal Law backing).
- **Safety:** Accident liability (Mitigation: Mandatory Insurance check during onboarding).
- **Liquidity:** Chicken-and-egg problem (Mitigation: Launch city-by-city).
